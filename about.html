About Us

Your trusted source for quality chicken products in Kenya

Our Story

Founded in 2020, KenChick Market has grown from a small local supplier to one of Kenya's most trusted sources of quality chicken products. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide fresh, high-quality chicken products to Kenyan households while supporting local farmers.

Today, we work directly with carefully selected local farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainable farming practices. This direct relationship ensures that we can offer the freshest products while maintaining competitive prices.

Our Values

Quality

We maintain the highest standards in our products, ensuring that every item meets our strict quality criteria.

Community

We support local farmers and contribute to the growth of our community through various initiatives.
